**아키텍처 결정(AD)이란 무엇인가?**

- 프로젝트 초기에 올바르게 내리고 싶은 결정 (M. Fowler,[IEEE Software 기사](http://ieeexplore.ieee.org/xpl/articleDetails.jsp?arnumber=1231144)).
- 변경하기 비용이 많이 드는 설계 결정 (G. Booch,[SATURN 2016 기조연설](https://saturn2016.sched.org/event/63lK/keynote-abstracting-the-unknown)).
- 더 정교한 정의: 아키텍처 결정은 시스템의 비기능적 특성을 직접적으로 또는 간접적으로 결정합니다. 각 결정은 여러 잠재적 해결책(옵션)이 존재하는 구체적이고 아키텍처적으로 중요한 설계 이슈(설계 문제)를 설명하며, 원하는 품질 속성을 참조하는 등의 방법으로 선택된 해결책의 선택에 대한 근거를 제공합니다. 아키텍처 결정은 소프트웨어 시스템 전체 또는 그러한 시스템의 하나 이상의 핵심 구성 요소와 관련됩니다. 예시로는 프로그래밍 언어와 도구의 선택, 아키텍처 패턴의 선택, 통합 기술의 선택, 미들웨어 자산의 선택 등이 있습니다. 또한 참조하세요[이 글](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Architectural_decision)위키백과에서 그리고[이 이야기](https://medium.com/olzzio/from-architectural-decisions-to-design-decisions-f05f6d57032b)미디엄에서.
- 다음에서 광고를 만들어야 합니다[가장 책임감 있는 순간 (MRM)](http://wirfs-brock.com/blog/2011/01/18/agile-architecture-myths-2-architecture-decisions-should-be-made-at-the-last-responsible-moment/)그리고 결정이 다음을 만족할 때 중단합니다[완료 정의](https://docsoc.medium.com/a-definition-of-done-for-architectural-decisions-426cf5a952b9).
- 더 많은 정의는 소프트웨어 아키텍처 서적과 학술 논문에서 찾을 수 있습니다.

# [Architectural Knowledge Management (AKM)](https://www.ost.ch/en/research-and-consulting-services/computer-science/ifs-institute-for-software-new/cloud-application-lab)

AKM and Architectural Decision (AD) Research at HSR/OST

[Articles on architectural decisions](https://ozimmer.ch/tags/#architectural-decisions) in [«The Concerned Architect»](https://ozimmer.ch/tags/#architecture) blog. Example: «[ADR = Any Decision Record? Architecture, Design and Beyond»](https://ozimmer.ch/practices/2021/04/23/AnyDecisionRecords.html)

**Goals:**

- Contribute to body of software architecture knowledge (both general and style-specific knowledge: SOA, cloud, microservices)
- Transfer research results from [Software Architectural Knowledge Management (SAKM)](http://www.springer.com/us/book/9783642023736) research community to practice
- Propose and empirically validate simple but powerful techniques for architectural decision identification, making, enactment - and integrate them into agile practices and tools (e.g., decision backlog, architecturally evident coding styles)

- [ADMentor](https://ifs.hsr.ch/index.php?id=13201&L=4#c48848), prototypical tool support for architectural decision modelling and reuse, and Cloud Design. **What is an Architectural Decision (AD)?**

- A decision that you wish you could get right early in a project (M. Fowler, in an [IEEE Software article](http://ieeexplore.ieee.org/xpl/articleDetails.jsp?arnumber=1231144)).
- A design decision that is costly to change (G. Booch, [SATURN 2016 keynote](https://saturn2016.sched.org/event/63lK/keynote-abstracting-the-unknown)).
- A more elaborate definition: Architectural decisions directly or indirectly determine the non-functional characteristics of a system. Each decision describes a concrete, architecturally significant design issue (design problem) for which several potential solutions (options) exist, and provides rationale for the selection of the chosen solution(s), e.g., by referencing desired quality attributes. Architectural decisions concern a software system as a whole, or one or more of the core components of such a system. Examples are the selection of programming languages and tools, of architectural patterns, of integration technologies, and of middleware assets. Also see [this article](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Architectural_decision) in Wikipedia and [this story](https://medium.com/olzzio/from-architectural-decisions-to-design-decisions-f05f6d57032b) on Medium.
- Make sure to make ADs at the [Most Responsible Moment (MRM)](http://wirfs-brock.com/blog/2011/01/18/agile-architecture-myths-2-architecture-decisions-should-be-made-at-the-last-responsible-moment/) and stop when a decision satisfies its [Definition of Done](https://docsoc.medium.com/a-definition-of-done-for-architectural-decisions-426cf5a952b9).
- More definitions can be found in software architecture books and scientific papers.
